Meet The Health Office Staff:



  • Krista Haley RN,BSN
    Chief Medical Officer


  • I have been a nurse for over 23 years and have had the privilege of being the Director of Clinical Quality Assurance, supervising care, and providing education for 17 physicians, nurses, and staff for five medical offices and in 4 hospitals in the U.S. I have also provided care in cardiology, ICU, ER, medical-surgical, oncology, and other hospital units. I have lived in Ethiopia for over six years, providing medical education and care in Addis and remote villages with limited access. I have been married to my wonderful husband, Mark, for over 24 years and have three fabulous sons. It is my pleasure and privilege to be a part of such a wonderful ICS community, and I absolutely love getting to care for each student and staff on campus.
