Health Office Services


• Assess and treat illness or injury for all students & staff

• Provide monitoring and treatment at all sport events and after school activities

• Monitor and treat chronic health conditions

• Answer parent questions via email and phone

• Provide contact tracing to all students and staff if Covid positive case occurs

• Monitor all lab results for Covid tests needed due to symptoms or travel

• Administer medication

• Monitor illness “outbreaks”

• Vision, Dental and Lice screenings.

• “Emotional band-aids”

• Maintain health records of all students

• Provide medical support on field trips

• CPR, BLS, First Aid, and First Responder training.

• Offer classes: health, puberty, hygiene, anatomy and more

• Cafeteria and canteen inspections

• Organize health-related events.

Health Office Location
